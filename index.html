A little bit about me.

I'm a small suburban town girl living with my family in the huge region the world knows as "Silicon Valley." Originally aiming for a career in mass media, I discovered the wonderful wide world of the web just about a year before I earned my BA degree as a new form of medium alongside print, TV, and the radio. But rather than using this new medium for content and information to the masses, I became more interested in the early stages of building this new medium than filling in the blanks. It was that time that I delved myself deeper into web design and development.

I have many hobbies and interests and I made side projects and mock sites based on those interests. It was then that I discovered that I do have the inspiration and the means of building websites, not just for myself, but for everyone who wants one or needs one. By word of mouth, I have been building and maintaining sites for others, as well as being able to write about my journeys and teaching others the passion as I do.s

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
